UN Women Committee

Model United Nations Conference

1. Introduction

Simulation of UN Women’s work to promote gender equality and empower women and girls globally.

2. Mandate & Objectives

  • Promote gender equality and women’s rights
  • Support women’s economic, political, and leadership empowerment
  • Eliminate violence against women and girls
  • Coordinate gender mainstreaming across UN agencies and partners

3. Committee Structure

  • Membership: UN Member States and stakeholder representatives
  • Leadership: Chair, Vice-Chairs.

4. Typical Session Flow

  1. Roll call and agenda adoption
  2. General debate
  3. Interactive hearings with experts/NGOs
  4. Caucusing (formal and informal)
  5. Drafting resolutions

5. Draft Resolution Components

  • Preambulatory clauses citing CEDAW, Beijing Declaration, and key conventions
  • Operative clauses outlining programs, legal reforms, and monitoring

7. Delegate Preparation & Strategy

  • Research national gender policies and best practices
  • Draft position papers with UN Women data
  • Collaborate with NGOs and civil society
  • Use case studies to support proposals

8. Expected Outcomes

  • Resolutions advancing gender-responsive policies
  • Enhanced understanding of gender mainstreaming
  • Stronger networks with stakeholders

9. Conclusion

Empower delegates to champion gender equality and draft impactful resolutions.
